Quant Formula Sheet

Percentages, profit-loss, time-work, speed-distance, averages, ratios, and simple interest — all at a glance.

  • Percentage change = (change ÷ original) × 100
  • Speed = Distance ÷ Time
  • Average = Sum of values ÷ Count
  • SI = (P × R × T) ÷ 100

Verbal Practice Tips
